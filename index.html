Brain.io
The Path to Paradigm Shift
About
Brain.io is a startup research, development and innovation company based in The Grand Duchy of Luxembourg working on four major areas;
  • 1.Open Research
    (Information Hub) A collaborative approach for open research communities.
  • 2.Information Technology
    (Common Brain) Synthetic Intelligent Cloud Architecture for the future of internet.
  • 3.Collective Awareness
    (Manifesto) A collective awareness framework for policy making, standardization, accreditation and compliance.
  • 4.Biophysics
    (Human Extensibility) Human bioinformatics algorithms for better health.
Introduction
Our research areas are diverse yet our projects are interconnected deeply, the success of one research depends on the progress of the others. We believe that the combination of those aspects of science would lead to paradigm shifts in all fields of science.
1.Information Hub
RESEARCH MISSION
_The Abstract
Researching a methodology to conduct open research using a collaborative approach designed for multi-disciplinary interventions, multi-scope goal alignment, advanced analytics and a unified research experience for international cooperation.
_The Framework
Developing an open research framework that utilize the open research methodology to boost engagement of open scientific communities to achieve common goals.
_The Community
  • Community participation in the open research.
  • Community contribution to the open libraries.
  • Community contribution in defining policies for public domain information.
RESEARCH VISION
We aim to become a catalyst for innovation by providing services and support to the open research community.

2.The Common Brain
RESEARCH MISSION
_The Abstract
Researching an open source intelligent architecture for future internet, that is deeply sustainable over a highly distributed hybrid network, deeply integrated self-governed, heterogenous by design and logical by nature.
_The Framework
Developing a logic-driven prototyping framework with a deeply integrable component ecosystem, built-in development and runtime environments with deployment management, aiming to achieve an unprecedented user experience for SMEs and Individuals in application creation.
_The Community
  • Community contribution in applications in the ecosystem.
  • Community participation in the experience.
RESEARCH VISION
Deploying the common brain across the world and providing intelligence as a service in every aspect of technology and hoping to achieve perfect harmony by bridging the technological differences between different technological stacks.

4.Human Extensibility
RESEARCH MISSION
.The Abstract
The establishment of the scientific ground for a field of science concerned with the study of the physics and physiology of the human being, to provide techniques and genetic algorithms for human extensibility.
.The Framework
The engagement of human extensibility with the Intelligent Cloud Architecture to establish a framework that facilitate the comprehension of the human genome and its interactions.
.The Community
  • Community contribution to the Open Libraries.
  • Community contribution in defining policies, standards and regulations.
  • Community participation in the research.
RESEARCH VISION
  • Simulation Framework
    Framework that simulate changes and interactions of the human genome based on the Logic and Experience from the Open Libraries.
  • Health Mentor
    Health mentor that builds your health profile, monitor your daily activities and provide health mentoring.
  • Diagnostic Service
    Human Genome Cloud Diagnostic Service for the Health Industry
RESEARCH GOALS AND EXPECTED IMPACTS
.Fundamental Knowledge
  • Better Understanding of Health and Diseases
    An approach to understand health by defining normal gene functionality and its variations.
  • Better Understanding of Environmental Effects
    An approach to understand changes in gene expression driven by environmental effects.
.Techniques and Algorithms
  • Bioinformatics Algorithms
    An algorithms to measure the interaction between environmental effects and gene mutation.
  • Natural Techniques for Better Mutations
    Solutions combined of Nutritions, Physical Activities, physical effects and Parasympathetic status to achieve higher quality mutations
.Health Schemes
  • Prevention Schemes
    Healthy aging plans to prevent potential diseases via natural techniques.
  • Improvement Schemes
    Self Improvement programs to increase system performance and consequently pass better genes to descendants.
  • Health Promotion Schemes
    Increased awareness in human extensibility.
